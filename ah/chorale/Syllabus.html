Course Pre-requisites, Co-requisites, and/or Other Restrictions

This is a non-audition choir. However to enjoy this singing experience, student singers should have the ability to match pitches and the ability to learn their part and sing in an ensemble. Recordings and midi-files may be provided or recommended to help. Students will be expected to attend and participate in ALL classes/rehearsals.

Course Description

This class is designed for student singers at UTD, for college credit, and as an outreach to singers from the surrounding communities as a part of UTD outreach to the community. This semester UTD CC will perform a wide variety of music.

ATTENDANCE POLICY

Rehearsals and performances: Attendance at all performances and rehearsals is mandatory. The concerts take the place of a final examination. Additional rehearsals will be announced well in advance. Attendance is required and will affect the course grade. You are allowed one unexcused absence. You may attend musical concerts here at UTD or in the Dallas metroplex and submit a two-page review of the concert to make up no more than four absences (see below). Please don’t abuse this privilege; I really need you in here the majority of the time. Please be on time for rehearsals; our time together is limited and must be used efficiently. Late arrival will not be tolerated. If you are not there when I take attendance, you will be marked absent.

Student Learning Objectives/Outcomes

Students will demonstrate mastery of musical notation in choral music. Students will employ effective vocal technique within a choral setting. Students will demonstrate understanding of the stylistic elements of musical interpretation and employ them in their performance of advanced repertoire.

Required Textbooks and Materials

You will need a black, 3-ring binder with pockets for this class. Please purchase it by the second class session. All original music issued for this course is the property of UTD and must be returned in good condition. You will be expected to pay the replacement cost of any missing or damaged music. Please remember that music scores are very expensive and can be difficult to replace. Please be careful! Use pencil only to mark original scores; no pens or highlighters, please! If you wish to use highlighters, please copy the music at your own expense. Please bring a pencil to every class to make notes about direction, text, etc.

Assignments & Academic Calendar

This is a performance course. Attendance at rehearsals and concerts is mandatory and replaces exams and assignments. Please check your schedule now – work, other activities, travel, etc. are not an excuse to miss rehearsal. No exceptions.

You are encouraged to strengthen your choral ear, by attending other choral/musical/ UTD concerts. You may choose the concert from the UTD Arts and Humanities event list at http://www.utdallas.edu/ah/events/. It must be a concert event (not a play or dance show). These concerts are free to UTD students. Off-campus performances may be substituted only with prior approval of the instructor. If you choose to attend concerts off-campus, the admission cost is your responsibility.

Grading Policy

Grades are based on the following: Attendance (including concerts), 80%, Concert reports 20%. Each unexcused absence will result in a deduction of ½ of a letter grade.

Course & Instructor Policies

I follow all University policies and procedures as listed below. Absences will be excused for only the following and with the following documentation. Notification of absences MUST BE IN WRITING. All other absences are unexcused and will affect your course grade. Pleased keep me informed of any circumstances that will impair your ability to sing.

  • Illness: Doctor’s Note
  • UTD Activity: Letter from faculty member/coach/club advisor
  • Family emergency: Letter from student with explanation (ie death in the family, etc)
  • Religious holy days: As determined by University policy.
