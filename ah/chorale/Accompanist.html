Jordan Peek

Community Chorale Accompanist

A native of DeKalb, Texas, Jordan Peek is the Associate Director of Music and Organist at All Saints Catholic Church in Dallas, Texas.  He is also an active accompanist at many area schools.  Jordan began piano in third grade and organ in middle school.  He completed a Bachelor of Music degree at Baylor University where he studied with well-known recitalist Dr. Joyce Jones.  He holds a Master of Music degree from The University of Texas at Austin where he studied organ and sacred music with Drs. Gerre and Judith Hancock.  Other teachers include Dr. Bradley Welch and Larry Dymott.  Jordan place first in the Texas Music Teachers Association solo organ competition and received Honorable Mention in the 2008 William Hall Organ Competition in San Antonio.   In July 2011, Jordan was featured in a recital at the Basilica de Santa Maria del Coroin San Sebastian, Spain.  He is beginning his second year as accompanist of the University of Texas at Dallas Community Chorale.
